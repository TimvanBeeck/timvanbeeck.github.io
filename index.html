About me

Welcome to my page! I am a PhD Student in Mathematics at the University of Göttingen focusing on numerical and applied mathematics, in particular on numerical methods for partial differential equations. Since 2021, I support research and teaching activities in the working group Computational PDEs. More broadly, I am interested in the application of mathematical techniques in geometry processing, data analysis and finance.
